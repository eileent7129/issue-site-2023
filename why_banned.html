Why was the course banned in Florida?

"Florida's Department of Education has rejected an Advanced Placement course covering African American sudies -- saying the class indoctrinates students to 'a political agenda'." - NPR

The state of Florida is among many others that are taking action to restrict edcuational topics revolving around race, sexual orientation, and gender identity. Florida Commissioner of Education called the course a "woke indoctrination masquerading as education". In his Twitter thread, he points out his topicsa of concern in the curriculum, which include intersectionality, black queer studies, movements for Black lives, and the reparations movement.

The banning of the AP course is not the only step the state of Flord has taken to limit was is taught in its classrooms. Other acts like the "Stop Woke Act" and the "Parental Rights in Education" bill restrict educational topics of raced, sexual orientation, and gender identity from the classroom and give parents the right to sue teachrs if they choose to teach such a curriculum.
